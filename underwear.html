How to Use & Care

check_circleHow to Use

  1. Wear: Simply wear them like you would your regular underwear on your period days.
  2. Absorb: Depending on your flow, you can wear them alone or as a backup with a cup or tampon.
  3. Change: Change them based on your flow, similar to how you would with a pad or tampon. Most can last up to 12 hours.

local_laundry_serviceCleaning & Care

  1. Rinse: After use, rinse the underwear under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Wash: Toss them in the washing machine on a delicate/cold cycle. Avoid fabric softener and bleach.
  3. Dry: Hang them to dry. Avoid using a machine dryer as high heat can damage the absorbent layers.
