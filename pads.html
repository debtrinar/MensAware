What Are Reusable Pads?

Reusable menstrual pads are a sustainable alternative to disposable pads. Made from soft, absorbent fabrics like cotton, bamboo, or hemp, they can be washed and reused for years. They offer a comfortable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective way to manage your period with kindness to your body and the planet.

How to Use Them

It's as simple as one, two, three. Here's a quick guide.

1. Place & Snap

Place the pad in your underwear, soft side up. Fold the wings around and snap them shut. The fit should be snug and comfortable.

2. Change as Needed

Change your pad every 4-6 hours, or as you would a disposable. This depends on your personal flow and comfort.

3. Store On-the-Go

Fold the used pad inwards, snap it shut, and store it in a waterproof "wet bag" until you get home to wash it.

Cleaning & Care

Caring for your pads is easy and ensures they last for years. A little care goes a long way!

  • check_circle Rinse: After use, rinse with cold water until the water runs clear. This prevents stains from setting.
  • check_circle Wash: Toss them in your regular laundry with a gentle detergent. Avoid fabric softeners and bleach as they can reduce absorbency.
  • check_circle Dry: Air dry for best results, either on a line or flat. Sunlight is a natural sanitizer and stain remover!
