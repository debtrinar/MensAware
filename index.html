About Us: Our Mission

MensaAware is dedicated to fostering self-awareness around menstrual health and hygiene. We believe knowledge is the first step towards empowerment, well-being, and dismantling long-held taboos.

What We Do

We provide accessible education, bust myths with scientific facts, promote eco-friendly menstrual products, and create supportive communities to ensure everyone can manage their menstruation with dignity.

Myths vs. Facts

Let's debunk common myths with science-backed facts. Click on a myth to reveal the truth.

Myth: You can't get pregnant on your period. expand_more

Fact: While unlikely, it is possible. Sperm can survive in the reproductive tract for up to five days, so if you have a shorter cycle and ovulate soon after your period, conception could occur.

Myth: Period syncing with friends is a real thing. expand_more

Fact: Despite popular belief, there's no scientific evidence to support this. Studies have shown that any perceived syncing is likely due to mathematical coincidence and the natural variability of menstrual cycles.

Myth: Exercising on your period is harmful. expand_more

Fact: Quite the opposite! Gentle exercise can actually help alleviate cramps and boost your mood by releasing endorphins. It's important to listen to your body and choose activities that feel right for you.
